# Usage & Walkthrough

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to utilize ValidationEngine to test an idea.

## Step 1: Submitting an Idea
1. Open up the **Dashboard**.
2. Locate the "Start A Validation Run" card.
3. Enter a specific, context-rich idea. Example: *"An AI tool that scrapes GitHub repositories to generate automated documentation for legacy codebases."*
4. Click **Generate Validation Engine**. Read the tooltips as the background process runs.

## Step 2: Interpreting the Research
Once the loader completes, the dashboard will render "Research With Sources".
You will see several cards. These aren't made up; they represent actual search results gathered instantly against your idea. Read their positioning to understand your competitors.

## Step 3: Launching the Prototype
Look at the Sidebar to find the `Live Page` URL. The backend has constructed a public website for your startup dynamically.
- Click **Open Live Page**.
- Copy the URL, and mock sharing it to a community.
- Enter an email to sign up for the waitlist. 

## Step 4: The Decision Engine Verdict
Return to the Dashboard and watch the **Decision Engine** section immediately update. Depending on your mocked traffic and signup counts, the engine will assign a classification:
- **Collect Data**: Keep pushing the URL, insufficient visitors.
- **Pivot**: Low conversion rates. The problem isn't resonating.
- **Kill**: Zero traction after significant visits. Abandon the build.
- **Go**: High conversion and interview requests. Start coding immediately!
