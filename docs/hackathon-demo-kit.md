# Hackathon Demo Kit

## 10-Second Hook

"Most AI tools help founders write plans. This one tells you whether the idea deserves to exist by turning it into a live market test."

## Problem

Founders usually validate ideas with scattered tools:

- one tool for market research
- another for copy
- another for landing pages
- another for analytics

The result is noise instead of evidence. Teams still do not know whether real users care.

## Live Demo Flow

1. Open the dashboard and paste a rough startup idea.
2. Show the sourced research cards with URLs and pricing.
3. Show the generated hypothesis and experiment plan.
4. Open the live landing page.
5. Submit one waitlist signup with an interview request.
6. Return to the dashboard and show:
   - visitor count
   - waitlist signups
   - conversion rate
   - evidence state
   - Go / Pivot / Kill recommendation

## What To Emphasize

- The product is grounded because research includes source links.
- The output is deployable because the landing page is actually live.
- The verdict is evidence-based because it reacts to traffic and signups.

## Ending Pitch

"AI should not stop at generating ideas. It should help founders falsify weak ideas early and double down on strong ones. This turns startup validation into a repeatable system."

## Winning Version

AI Startup Validation Engine helps founders decide what is worth building. It takes one raw idea, gathers market evidence, creates a testable hypothesis, launches a live landing page, captures real waitlist demand, and converts those signals into a blunt Go, Pivot, or Kill recommendation.
